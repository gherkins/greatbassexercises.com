How to use this app
  • Choose an exercise from the dropdown.
  • Turn on Audio(!).
  • Hit the play/stop button to toggle playback.
  • Use the slider to change the tempo.
  • Play along and enjoy.

The lowest fretnumber is shown in the top left corner of the fretboard.
Below the fretboard you can check/uncheck the bars you want to play.

You can also click/tap on the bar indicator above the checkbox to jump to that bar.
