How to use this app
  1. Choose an exercise from the dropdown
  2. Hit the play/stop button to toggle playback
  3. Use the slider to change the tempo
  4. Play along and enjoy

The lowest fret is shown in the top left corner of the fretboard.

Below the fretboard you can check/uncheck the bars you want to play.
You can also click/tap on the bar indicator above the checkbox to jump to that bar.
